03 Process

Three steps from discovery to delivery.

i.

Discover & Inquire

Browse verified factories by category. Submit a single inquiry — our team routes it to the right makers and follows up within 48 hours. No login. No subscription.

ii.

Quote & Sample

Receive quotes directly from the workshop floor. Order paid samples with full traceability. Our Foshan team can audit production in person before you commit.

iii.

Order & Ship

Place orders via our Hong Kong entity. Pay by T/T to DBS Bank in your local currency. We handle export documentation, QC, and consolidation through our own warehouses.
