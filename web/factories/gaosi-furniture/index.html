04About

A family workshop that grew up with Foshan's furniture cluster.

Founded in 2008 by master craftsman Wang Jiamin, Gaosi Furniture began as a 400 m² workshop producing solid oak dining sets for the domestic Chinese market. Over the next 18 years, the workshop grew alongside the Lecong furniture cluster — Foshan's mid-to-high tier production heartland — eventually expanding to a 12,000 m² facility with full vertical integration from kiln-drying to finishing.

Today Gaosi specializes in solid wood and engineered furniture for European buyers, with a particular focus on Scandinavian-aesthetic case goods. The workshop holds OEM contracts with three Nordic furniture brands and supplies hospitality projects across Northern Europe.

What sets Gaosi apart from larger Foshan factories is its in-house finishing line — a four-stage hand-applied oil and wax process that produces the matte, natural look favored by European designers. This is the reason most clients return for repeat orders.

The workshop is BSCI audited and FSC certified for both FM and CoC. Sample lead time is 14 days; production runs of 500–1,000 units take 35–45 days; full container loads ship FOB Shenzhen via weekly consolidation.

05Story

Eighteen years on the workshop floor.

2008

Workshop founded

Wang Jiamin opens a 400 m² workshop in Lecong, Foshan, producing solid oak dining sets for the domestic Chinese market.

2013

First export order

First container of dining tables ships to a German importer. Workshop begins building a dedicated export production line.

2017

FSC certification

Achieves FSC Chain of Custody certification, opening path to large European retailers and hospitality projects.

2019

Facility expansion

Moves to current 12,000 m² facility with in-house kiln drying, CNC line, and dedicated finishing department.

2022

Scandinavian OEM partnerships

Signs OEM agreements with two Nordic furniture brands, establishing a dedicated production cell for Scandinavian-spec products.
