02Capabilities

From hand-blown glass to hotel-spec chandeliers.

/ 001

Hand-Blown Glass

In-house glass blowing studio for pendant shades, chandelier droplets, and custom glass forms. Clear, opal, amber, and custom colours. Annealing kiln ensures thermal stability. Batch colour matching to Pantone or physical samples.

/ 002

Metal Fabrication

Brass, steel, and aluminium fabrication for frames, arms, and backplates. CNC turning, bending, and welding. Brass spinning for dome and cone shades. Tolerance to ±0.5mm on all critical dimensions.

/ 003

Surface Finishing

Brushed brass, antique brass, polished chrome, matte black, and copper plating. Powder coating for industrial pendants. PVD coating for premium finishes. Salt spray testing to 240 hours on exterior wall lights.

/ 004

Crystal Cutting

K9 crystal precision cutting and polishing for chandelier droplets and beads. Custom crystal shapes and sizes. Machine-cut and hand-polished options. Lead-free crystal available for EU and US markets.

/ 005

Shade Making

Fabric, rattan, and paper shade production in-house. Linen, cotton, and silk fabrics in custom colours. Hand-woven rattan in natural and stained finishes. Shade frames welded and powder coated on site.

/ 006

Electrical Assembly

E14, E27, E26, and G9 socket assembly with CE and UL listed components. 3-core braided cable in custom colours. Inline switches, dimmers, and pull cords. 100% dielectric testing on every fitting.

/ 007

Hotel Specification

Hotel-grade finishing and packaging. Individual bubble wrapping and foam inserts. Installation instructions and mounting hardware included. Custom labels and hang tags from 50 pieces per SKU.

/ 008

Custom Design

Custom lighting design from sketch or reference image. 3D rendering and prototype within 21 days. Mould making for unique glass or metal forms. Exclusive designs protected by NDA on request.

04About

Zhongshan-made decorative lighting for interior designers and hoteliers.

Yimei Decorative Lighting was founded in 2014 in Guzhen, Zhongshan, the district that produces more decorative lighting than any other place on earth. The company began with metal industrial pendants for the domestic market, then invested in a hand-blown glass studio in 2016 after seeing growing demand for artisanal glass shades from European buyers.

The 14,000 m² facility now houses glass blowing, metal fabrication, crystal cutting, shade making, and final assembly. Every stage of production is managed in-house, which allows Yimei to control quality and offer custom finishes, colours, and sizes that would be impossible with a fully outsourced model. The company employs 30 glass blowers and 15 metalworkers.

Certification includes UL 1598 and ETL for North America, CE for Europe, and SAA for Australia. The hotel specification division produces custom chandeliers and wall sconces for boutique hotels, restaurants, and retail chains, with projects completed in the UK, US, and Australia.

Sample lead time is 21 days for custom designs and 14 days for standard lines. Production lead time is 30–45 days. Minimum order is 50 pieces per SKU for standard products and 100 pieces for custom designs. Exclusive designs are protected by NDA.

05Story

Eleven years crafting light one shade at a time.
