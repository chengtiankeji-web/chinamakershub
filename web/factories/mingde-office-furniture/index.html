03 About Mingde

22 Years of Office Furniture Excellence

Founded in 2002 in Dongguan's manufacturing hub, Mingde Office Furniture began as a small workshop specializing in handcrafted executive desks for local businesses. Today, we stand as one of China's leading commercial office furniture manufacturers, serving global corporate clients across North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific.

Our commitment to ergonomic design and manufacturing precision has earned us BIFMA certification — the gold standard for office furniture safety and performance. Every product leaves our 28,000 m² facility after rigorous quality control testing, ensuring compliance with international standards and client expectations.

We pride ourselves on our vertically integrated production model, controlling every stage from raw material sourcing to final assembly. This integration allows us to maintain consistent quality while offering competitive pricing and flexible lead times for OEM partnerships with global office furniture brands.

At Mingde, sustainability is integral to our operations. We source FSC-certified wood materials, implement water-based finishing systems, and maintain a zero-waste manufacturing policy, diverting 98% of production waste from landfills through recycling and repurposing programs.

22 Years of Continuous Growth

Secured first international OEM contract with European office furniture brand, expanding production to 8,000 m² facility.
