02Capabilities

Electronics manufacturing from component placement to finished product.

/ 001

Surface-Mount Technology

High-speed SMT lines with 0201 component capability and ±0.035mm placement accuracy. BGA, QFN, and CSP handling with X-ray verification. Nitrogen reflow for improved solder joint reliability. Lead-free and tin-lead processes available.

/ 002

Through-Hole Assembly

Manual and selective soldering for through-hole components. Wave soldering with nitrogen blanket for high-volume runs. Component forming, trimming, and clinching in-house. Mixed technology boards with SMT and TH on the same panel.

/ 003

Inspection and Testing

AOI, X-ray, and AXI for solder joint and component verification. ICT with bed-of-nails and flying probe options. Functional testing with custom test fixtures and firmware upload. Burn-in testing for reliability validation.

/ 004

Rigid-Flex and HDI

Rigid-flex PCB assembly with polyimide and FR-4 hybrid construction. HDI microvia technology with 3/3 mil line/space. Controlled impedance and depth drilling. IPC Class 3 acceptance for medical and aerospace applications.

/ 005

Cable and Harness

Custom wire harness assembly with UL listed wire and connectors. Crimping, soldering, and overmoulding in-house. IPC/WHMA-A-620 Class 2 and Class 3 acceptance. 100% continuity and pull-force testing on all harnesses.

/ 006

Box-Build and Integration

Mechanical assembly integrating PCBAs, enclosures, cables, and displays. Torque-controlled fastening and adhesive bonding. Full functional testing including calibration and firmware. Custom packaging and labelling for retail.

/ 007

Component Sourcing

BOM analysis and component sourcing with authorised distributor networks. Counterfeit avoidance through AVL management and incoming inspection. Buffer stock for long-lead-time components. Cost optimisation through alternative part recommendations.

/ 008

Quality Systems

IATF 16949 for automotive, ISO 13485 for medical, and ISO 9001 for general electronics. IPC-A-610 and IPC/WHMA-A-620 workmanship standards. Full traceability from component lot to finished serial number. First article inspection on all new products.

04About

Shenzhen-made electronics for consumer, automotive, and medical device makers.

Xinte Electronics was founded in 2015 in Shenzhen, at the centre of the world's electronics supply chain. The company began as an SMT assembly house for local consumer electronics brands, then expanded into DIP, testing, and full box-build as customers requested more integrated manufacturing services.

The 12,000 m² facility houses eight SMT lines, four DIP stations, AOI and X-ray inspection, ICT and functional test areas, and a dedicated box-build assembly zone. All processes are under one roof, which allows Xinte to control quality and lead time without coordinating multiple subcontractors.

Quality certifications cover the full spectrum of target markets. IPC-A-610 Class 2 and Class 3 workmanship standards apply to all products. IATF 16949 opens the automotive sector. ISO 13485 covers medical device manufacturing. UL recognition allows products to carry the UL mark for North American safety compliance.

Prototype turnaround is five days from Gerber and BOM approval. Volume production capacity exceeds five million assembled pieces per year. Export markets include the United States, Germany, and the United Kingdom, serving IoT startups, automotive Tier 2 suppliers, and medical device companies.

05Story

Ten years building electronics from prototype to million-piece production.
