02Capabilities

From LED chip to certified luminaire.

/ 001

LED SMT Assembly

High-speed SMT lines for LED PCB assembly. Samsung, Bridgelux, and Epistar LED sources. Reflow profiling for each product family. AOI inspection after every SMT run.

/ 002

Driver Sourcing & Test

Mean Well, Inventronics, and custom driver options. 100% burn-in testing at 85°C for 4 hours. THD, power factor, and surge protection verified on every batch.

/ 003

Die Casting & Extrusion

In-house aluminium die casting for UFO high-bay housings and flood light bodies. CNC machining for heat sink fins and mounting surfaces. Thermal simulation for each new design.

/ 004

Optical Design

PC and PMMA lens design for beam control. Type II, III, and V street light distributions. UGR calculation for panel and troffer products. IES files provided with every sample.

/ 005

Integrating sphere and goniophotometer in-house. LM-80, LM-79, and TM-21 testing. IES, LDT, and UGR files generated. Reports accepted by DLC and Energy Star.

/ 006

IP66 and IP65 spray testing. IK08 and IK10 impact testing. Salt spray to 720 hours for coastal applications. Thermal cycling from -40°C to +85°C.

/ 007

UL/DLC Certification

Full UL 1598 and DLC Premium certification support. Photometric and electrical data submitted directly to UL and DLC. Annual factory inspection passed every year since 2013.

/ 008

Custom wattages, CCTs, and beam angles from existing tooling. New mould design for unique housings. Private label and custom packaging from 200 pieces.

04About

Zhongshan-made LED lighting for warehouse, retail, and street applications.

Haoguang LED was founded in 2010 in Guzhen, Zhongshan — the district known as China's lighting capital. The company began producing fluorescent tube fixtures for the domestic market, then transitioned to LED luminaires in 2012 as the technology matured and export demand grew.

The 20,000 m² facility integrates SMT assembly, aluminium die casting, driver sourcing and testing, and photometric measurement. The in-house integrating sphere and goniophotometer allow Haoguang to generate LM-79 and IES files without relying on external labs, which reduces the time between prototype and DLC submission.

Certification is a core strength. The company holds UL 1598 and DLC Premium for the North American market, CE for Europe, and SAA for Australia and New Zealand. All LED sources are LM-80 tested and the in-house lab generates TM-21 lumen maintenance projections for every product family.

Sample lead time is 14 days with full photometric data. Production lead time is 28–40 days depending on volume and customisation. Annual capacity is 500,000 units across all product lines. Custom CCT, wattage, and beam angles are available from 200 pieces.

05Story

Fifteen years illuminating warehouses, streets, and retail spaces.

2010

Factory founded

Opens fluorescent fixture workshop in Guzhen, Zhongshan, producing T5 and T8 battens for the domestic market.

2013

UL and DLC certification

First LED high-bay range achieves UL 1598 and DLC Premium listing. North American warehouse lighting market opens up.

2016

LM-80 testing lab

In-house integrating sphere and goniophotometer commissioned. LM-80, LM-79, and IES file generation handled internally.

2018

SAA and European expansion

CE and SAA certification achieved for panel and troffer ranges. Australian and UK distribution contracts begin.

2021

Smart lighting systems

Launches DALI and Zigbee-compatible high-bay and panel ranges. Occupancy sensor and daylight harvesting options added.
