02Capabilities

Full-service packaging from print to finished box.

/ 001

Offset Printing

4-colour and 6-colour Heidelberg offset presses. Print resolution to 175 lpi. CMYK, Pantone, and spot colour matching. Pre-press proofing with digital colour accuracy to within 2 Delta E.

/ 002

Corrugated Converting

Rotary die cutting, flatbed die cutting, and flexo folder-gluer lines. E, B, C, and double-wall flute. FSC-certified kraft and white top liner. Auto-bottom, crash-lock, and standard RSC styles.

/ 003

Rigid Box Making

Semi-automatic rigid box lines for gift boxes, drawer boxes, and hinged lid boxes. 1.5–3mm greyboard with art paper, linen, or leatherette wrap. Corner rounding and ribbon attachment in-house.

/ 004

Post-Print Finishing

Hot foil stamping, embossing, debossing, spot UV, and matte/gloss lamination. Soft-touch and velvet laminates. Window patching and die-cutting. All finishing handled on site for quality control.

/ 005

Insert and Foam

Custom EVA, EPE, and paper pulp inserts. Die-cut foam inserts for electronics and cosmetics. Flocked and velvet-lined trays. Insert design service included with rigid box orders.

/ 006

Paper Bag Making

Twisted handle and rope handle bag lines. SOS, pinch-bottom, and luxury constructions. FSC kraft, art paper, and textured board. Reinforced bottom and handle attachment tested to 5kg load.

/ 007

Sustainability

FSC-certified board and paper throughout. Soy-based inks on all litho print. Recyclable and biodegradable options. Carbon footprint calculation available on request for corporate clients.

/ 008

Prototype and Dieline

7-day prototype from artwork approval. 3D mock-ups and white samples for structural approval. Dieline design and optimisation for material yield. Free structural design consultation.

04About

Dongguan-made packaging for cosmetics, electronics, and premium brands.

Longda Packaging was founded in 2009 in Dongguan, at the centre of China's packaging manufacturing corridor. The company began with corrugated shipping boxes for local electronics factories, then expanded into rigid gift boxes and paper bags as e-commerce and premium retail packaging demand grew.

The 18,000 m² facility houses offset printing, die cutting, laminating, foil stamping, and rigid box assembly. All post-print finishing — hot foil, embossing, spot UV, and soft-touch lamination — is handled in-house, which allows Longda to maintain tight colour and registration control without coordinating multiple subcontractors.

Sustainability is a growing focus. The company holds FSC chain-of-custody certification and offers soy-based inks, recyclable liners, and biodegradable alternatives. BSCI and Sedex audits are passed annually, making Longda suitable for European and North American retail brands with strict supply chain requirements.

Prototype lead time is 7 days from artwork approval. Production lead time is 15–25 days for standard orders. Volume capacity exceeds 50 million pieces per year across all product lines. Custom dieline design and structural optimisation are provided free of charge.

05Story

Sixteen years packaging products from warehouse to boutique shelf.

2009

Factory founded

Opens corrugated box workshop in Dongguan, producing shipping cartons for local electronics and toy manufacturers.

2012

FSC certification

Achieves FSC chain-of-custody certification. Begins supplying sustainable packaging to European cosmetics and food brands.

2015

Automatic die-cutting line

Installs automatic flatbed die-cutter and folder-gluer. Capacity increases to 20 million corrugated pieces per year.

2018

Rigid gift box line

Adds semi-automatic rigid box making for cosmetics and electronics gift packaging. Hot foil, embossing, and spot UV finishing in-house.

2021

Eco-friendly materials

Introduces soy-based inks, biodegradable laminates, and paper foam inserts. Carbon footprint reporting available for corporate clients.

2026

Joins ChinaMakersHub

Onboards as CMH workshop (CMH-F-LDP013), expanding buyer access through the platform's curated Greater Bay Area network.
