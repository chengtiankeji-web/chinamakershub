02Capabilities

From municipal street lighting to landscape and architectural flood.

/ 001

LED Module Assembly

High-power LED module assembly with Cree, Bridgelux, and Epistar sources. Reflow soldering and thermal interface material application. Module-level photometric testing before final assembly.

/ 002

Die Casting

Aluminium die casting for street light housings, flood light bodies, and garden luminaire bases. 400T and 800T machines. Powder coat and anodised finishes. Salt spray testing to 720 hours.

/ 003

Optical Design

PC and PMMA lens design for street light Type II, III, and V distributions. Flood light beam angles from 10° to 120°. Photometric files generated in-house for every product family.

/ 004

IP & IK Testing

In-house IP66 spray testing and IK08/IK10 impact testing. Thermal cycling and humidity testing. All outdoor products verified before shipment to ensure sealing integrity.

/ 005

Solar Integration

All-in-one solar street light design with monocrystalline panel, LiFePO4 battery, and MPPT controller. 3–5 rainy day autonomy. Microwave motion sensor and remote monitoring options.

/ 006

Smart Control

Photocell, microwave sensor, and DALI dimming options. NEMA socket compatible for remote monitoring. Programmable RGB scenes with DMX512 control for architectural flood lighting.

/ 007

Stadium Lighting

High-mast and stadium flood light design to 2000W. Asymmetric beam for uniform pitch illuminance. Flicker-free drivers for broadcast compliance. DIN 18015 and FIFA guidelines referenced.

/ 008

Project Support

Dialux lighting simulation and lux level calculation for municipal and commercial projects. Installation guidance and wiring diagrams. Spare parts availability for 10 years.

04About

Guangzhou-made outdoor lighting for municipal, commercial, and landscape projects.

Junhui Outdoor Lighting was founded in 2013 in Panyu, Guangzhou, a district with a long history of outdoor luminaire manufacturing. The company began with conventional street lights for municipal projects in southern China, then transitioned to LED in 2015 as city governments began retrofitting their lighting infrastructure.

The 18,000 m² facility covers LED module assembly, aluminium die casting, powder coating, and outdoor environmental testing. The company produces its own LED modules and drivers for standard ranges, which allows tight control over cost and lead time. Solar street light integration is handled in-house, from panel sizing to battery management.

Export markets are concentrated in the Middle East and Southeast Asia, where Junhui has supplied street lighting for municipal projects in Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Vietnam. CE and ENEC certification supports European projects, while SAA covers Australian and New Zealand requirements. All products are IP66 and IK10 rated as standard.

Sample lead time is 16 days with photometric data. Production lead time is 30–45 days depending on volume and customisation. Custom colours, wattages, and beam angles are available from 100 pieces. Dialux simulation and lux level calculation are provided free of charge for project orders.
