01 About

26 years building the displays that sell products on the shelf.

Founded in 2002 in Shenzhen's Pingshan district, Yixinheng (深圳市艺欣恒有机制品有限公司) has spent over two decades mastering a single material: acrylic. What began as a small fabrication shop has grown into a 12,000 m² full-capability operation serving retail brands across North America, Europe, and Japan.

The factory specializes in custom display solutions — the kind retail brands need when a standard off-the-shelf rack won't communicate their product story. Cosmetic counters, vape display towers, 3C electronics shelving, eyewear stands, supermarket gondola fixtures — Yixinheng builds all of it to spec, from a single prototype sample to full production runs.

With 26 years of production experience, a 12,000 m² facility, and a 14-day sample turnaround, Yixinheng serves OEM and ODM customers who need fast iteration and precise execution. The factory holds a foreign trade registration and handles export documentation directly — no trading company layer required.

Priority markets are the United States, Canada, UK, Germany, France, and Japan. Annual capacity stands at 80,000 units — equivalent to 200 full 40HQ containers — with MOQ set at 500 pieces or one 40HQ container.

05 Story

Twenty-six years on the fabrication floor.

2002

Factory established

Yixinheng opens in Pingshan, Shenzhen on July 9, producing acrylic sign holders and basic display fixtures for the domestic Chinese market.

2008

Export business begins

First export orders ship to North American retail brands. Factory registers for foreign trade and begins building English-language sales capability.

2015

Product line expansion

Expands from sign holders into full display solutions: cosmetic, vape, 3C electronics, and eyewear. Trophy and award customization line launched.

2020

OEM / ODM focus

Shifts production emphasis to full OEM and ODM service for international brands seeking custom-spec acrylic display solutions.

2026

Joins ChinaMakersHub network

Onboards as CMH verified workshop (CMH-F-3WQSUB), expanding direct buyer access for international brands seeking verified Greater Bay Area suppliers.
