04About

Metal furniture specialists in Guangzhou's manufacturing heartland.

Ruijin Metal Furniture was founded in 2010 in Guangzhou's Panyu district, producing steel shelving and display fixtures for domestic retail chains. The factory entered export markets in 2015 through a UK fashion brand commission for garment racks and display gondolas, and has since expanded its buyer base across Europe, North America, and Australia.

The 18,000 m² facility runs continuous steel fabrication, welding, and powder coat operations. The in-house 80-metre powder coat line handles the full production volume without outsourcing — a key advantage for buyers who specify custom RAL colours and need consistent colour matching across large orders and repeat batches months apart.

Ruijin's cast iron production capability is unusual for a GBA factory of this scale. The ability to produce complete cast iron table bases in-house — rather than sourcing from external foundries — gives Ruijin better control over dimensional accuracy and surface finish on what is typically a challenging component. Café furniture buyers in particular find this a meaningful differentiator.

Sample lead time is 12 days from approved drawing. Production runs of 500 units take 28–38 days FOB Guangzhou. Custom powder coat RAL colours are standard pricing on orders over 100 units.

05Story

Fifteen years forging furniture from raw steel.

2010

Factory founded

Opens steel fabrication workshop in Panyu, Guangzhou, producing shelving and storage fixtures for domestic retail chains.

2014

Powder coat expansion

Installs 80m continuous powder coat line. Colour consistency across large orders significantly improves. Custom RAL matching offered at standard pricing.

2015

First export shipment

UK fashion brand commissions garment racks and display gondolas. First container ships to London. Export production line formally established.

2018

Cast iron line

In-house cast iron production established. Café table base range launches. Complete table sets — cast iron base, tube frame, and top — now manufactured on one site.

2022

Laser cutting upgrade

6kW fibre laser cutting system installed. Sheet metal capability significantly expanded. Tighter tolerances and faster turnaround on sheet components.

2026

Joins ChinaMakersHub

Onboards as CMH verified workshop (CMH-F-RJM004), expanding direct buyer access through the platform's curated network.
