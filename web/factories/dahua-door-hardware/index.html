02Capabilities

Full-range hardware from casting to certification.

/ 001

Die Casting

Zinc alloy and brass die casting with 50+ CNC machines for post-cast machining. Tolerances to ±0.05mm on critical bearing and locking surfaces. Weekly capacity 200,000+ pieces.

/ 002

PVD Finishing

Physical vapour deposition coating in matte black, satin brass, brushed gold, and rose gold. Salt-spray tested to 720 hours. Vickers hardness HV 2,000+. Consistent across high-volume runs.

/ 003

Electroplating

Satin nickel, polished chrome, antique brass, and oil-rubbed bronze. RoHS-compliant plating chemistry throughout. Thickness verified by X-ray fluorescence (XRF) on every batch.

/ 004

ANSI Certification

Products certified to ANSI/BHMA A156.1 (butts and hinges), A156.2 (locks and lock trim), and A156.13 (mortise locks). Grade 1 across all categories. Cycle test reports available.

/ 005

EN Certification

CE marked to EN 1906 (building hardware — lever handles) and EN 1154 (door closers). Classification data sheets and DoP available for all CE-marked products.

/ 006

Custom OEM

Custom handle designs from 2D drawing or 3D file. 3D-printed prototype available within 5 days for approval before tooling. Minimum tooling charge from USD 800.

/ 007

Corrosion Testing

In-house salt spray test chamber to 720 hours. CASS (copper accelerated salt spray) also available. Full test report issued with each new product release and on request for existing lines.

/ 008

Retail Packaging

Custom blister pack, colour box, and polybag packaging. EAN barcode applied. Multilingual installation instructions included. Retailer-specific labelling accepted at 5,000+ unit orders.

04About

Foshan-made hardware certified to US, EU, and Australian codes.

Dahua Door Hardware was founded in 2009 in Foshan, which has been China's primary centre for architectural hardware manufacturing for over three decades. The company began producing OEM hinges and handles for domestic builders merchants before turning to export markets in 2013, when the first ANSI/BHMA certification was obtained for its hinge range.

The 16,000 m² facility covers the full hardware manufacturing process in-house: die casting in zinc alloy and brass, CNC machining, electroplating, PVD coating, mechanical assembly, and testing. The in-house salt spray chamber allows Dahua to test to 720 hours without relying on external labs, which reduces the time between product development and shipment for OEM customers.

Certification is a core part of Dahua's commercial proposition. The company is registered with the BHMA product directory for its ANSI-certified lines, CE marked to EN 1906 and EN 1154 for the European market, and certified to AS 4145 for Australia and New Zealand. All certification data sheets and declaration of performance documents are available for download from the CMH product page.

Sample lead time is 15 days. Custom OEM handle designs can be prototyped within 5 days using in-house 3D printing. Production lead time for standard items is 25–35 days; custom tooled items add 15–20 days for tooling approval before production.

05Story

Sixteen years building hardware to the standards that open doors.

2009

Factory founded

Opens die casting and electroplating workshop in Foshan, producing OEM hinges and handles for domestic builders merchants and hardware distributors.

2013

First ANSI certification

Achieves ANSI/BHMA A156.1 Grade 1 for the hinge range. Listed in the BHMA product directory. US builders merchant and distribution contracts begin.

2016

PVD line installed

In-house PVD coating line commissioned. Matte black, brushed gold, and rose gold finishes added to the range. Premium residential handle collections launch.

2018

CE marking achieved

EN 1906 and EN 1154 CE marking obtained. UK and European architectural hardware distribution channel opens up.
