04About

Foshan's hardware cluster — where European door & window hardware is made.

Dahua Door Hardware was founded in 2008 in Foshan's hardware manufacturing cluster — the core production base for China's mid-to-high end door & window hardware. The company began producing basic hinges and handles for the domestic market before pivoting to export in 2012, when it secured its first OEM contracts with UK and German construction hardware buyers.

The 12,000 m² facility integrates stamping, die-casting, surface treatment, and final assembly under one roof. All metal components undergo in-house salt spray testing to 480 hours — double the industry standard — which significantly reduces the risk of corrosion for hardware used in European coastal and high-humidity environments.

85% of production is now exported, with the UK and German construction markets accounting for the majority. The factory holds CE and ROHS certification for EU market access, and BS EN 1906 compliance for friction stays — critical for specifiers on European building projects. OEM/ODM is supported across all product lines, with no extra design fee for orders over 5000 units.

Sample lead time is 15 days from approved drawing, reflecting the precision required for hardware sampling relative to larger furniture components. Production runs of 1000–5000 units take 30–45 days FOB Guangzhou or Shenzhen.
