02Capabilities

Full-service sanitary ware from clay to finished bathroom.

/ 001

Vitreous China Forming

High-pressure casting and slip casting for toilets, basins, and cisterns. Computer-controlled plaster moulds with 0.3mm tolerance. Automated de-moulding and greenware handling for consistent wall thickness.

/ 002

Glazing and Firing

Robotic spray glazing with nano-ceramic coating for stain resistance and easy cleaning. Tunnel kiln firing to 1,250°C with computer-controlled temperature curves. Glaze hardness tested to Mohs 6.

/ 003

Tempered Glass Production

In-house 8mm tempered glass cutting, edging, and drilling for shower enclosures. CNC glass processing with polished edges and cut-outs. Thermal toughening to EN 12150 and AS/NZS 2208 standards.

/ 004

Hardware and Fittings

Chrome-plated brass and 304 stainless steel hinges, brackets, and wastes. PVD matte black and brushed nickel finishes available. All hardware salt-spray tested to 48 hours per ASTM B117.

/ 005

Smart Toilet Electronics

Electronic bidet seat assembly with instant heating, remote control, and sensor modules. IPX4 water resistance testing. CE and UL electrical safety certification for all electronic components.

/ 006

Quality Control and Testing

Water-tightness testing on 100% of toilets and basins. Flush performance testing to ASME A112.19.2 and EN 997. Load testing on shower trays to 150kg. CMM dimensional inspection on critical dimensions.

/ 007

Custom Mould Development

In-house mould design and CNC machining for custom shapes and sizes. 3D scanning of existing products for replica or improvement projects. Mould lead time 25–35 days for new designs.

/ 008

Packaging and Logistics

Individual foam wrapping and reinforced carton packaging for fragile ceramic and glass. Palletised loading with corner protection. FOB Shenzhen or Guangzhou with full export documentation.

04About

Foshan-made sanitary ware for residential, hotel, and commercial bathrooms.

Kaiwei Sanitary was founded in 2008 in Foshan, the heart of China's ceramic and sanitary ware production region. The company began with vitreous china toilets for the domestic market, then expanded into basins and shower enclosures as export demand grew.

The 20,000 m² facility includes high-pressure casting lines, robotic spray glazing, tunnel kilns, and tempered glass processing. All ceramic production — from clay preparation to kiln firing — is handled in-house, which gives Kaiwei tight control over glaze consistency, dimensional accuracy, and defect rates.

Quality certifications are comprehensive. CUPC approval opens the North American market. WELS certification covers Australian requirements. CE marking covers Europe. BSCI and SGS audits confirm social compliance and factory conditions for major retail buyers.

Export markets include the United States, Australia, and the Middle East. Hotel project experience spans three-star to five-star properties, with custom colours, logos, and packaging available. Mould development for custom shapes takes 25–35 days. Standard product sampling is 20 days from mould approval.

05Story

Seventeen years crafting bathrooms from clay to five-star hotel suites.

2008

Factory founded

Opens vitreous china toilet workshop in Foshan, supplying local builders and renovation contractors.

2011

CUPC certification

Achieves CUPC certification for North American market entry. Begins exporting wall-hung and back-to-wall toilets to the United States.

2014

Hotel project division

Launches dedicated hotel project division with custom colour, logo, and packaging services. First five-star hotel project in the Middle East.

2017

Smart toilet line

Adds electronic bidet seat and smart toilet assembly line. CE electrical safety certification for European smart toilet market.

2020

WELS certification

Achieves WELS water efficiency certification for Australian market. Expands basin and shower enclosure lines with tempered glass processing.
