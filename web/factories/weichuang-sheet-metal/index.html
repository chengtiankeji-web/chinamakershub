02Capabilities

Full-service fabrication from laser to powder coat.

/ 001

Laser Cutting

6kW fiber laser cutting for steel, stainless steel, and aluminium up to 20mm. Tolerance ±0.1mm. Nesting software optimises material yield. Rapid prototyping and production volumes on the same platform.

/ 002

CNC Punching

30-station CNC turret punch with auto tool changer. Hole, slot, and louvre patterns in a single setup. Ideal for perforated panels, enclosures, and chassis with repetitive patterns. Up to 3mm mild steel.

/ 003

CNC Bending

Multi-axis press brakes with robotic part handling. Bend angles to ±0.5°. Complex profiles with hems, offsets, and radii. Offline programming reduces setup time for prototype and short-run orders.

/ 004

Welding

MIG, TIG, and spot welding for steel, stainless steel, and aluminium. Welding procedure specifications (WPS) available. X-ray and dye penetrant inspection on structural welds. Certified welders per ISO 9606.

/ 005

Powder Coating

Automated powder coating line with pre-treatment. RAL and custom colour matching. Texture and gloss options. Salt spray testing to 500 hours. RoHS-compliant powders throughout.

/ 006

Anodising

Sulphuric acid anodising for aluminium panels and extrusions. Black, silver, gold, and blue colours. Hard anodising to 50μm for wear resistance. Laser etching of legends and logos post-anodise.

/ 007

Assembly

Mechanical assembly of enclosures, brackets, and chassis. PEM hardware insertion, riveting, and adhesive bonding. Full functional testing and dimensional inspection before shipment.

/ 008

Rapid Prototyping

7-day prototype turnaround from 3D model or drawing. No hard tooling required for prototypes under 50 pieces. Design for manufacture (DFM) feedback provided with every quote.

04About

Shenzhen precision sheet metal for electronics and industrial equipment.

Weichuang Sheet Metal was founded in 2011 in Shenzhen's Futian district, within walking distance of the electronics manufacturing cluster that surrounds Huaqiangbei. The company began as a laser cutting job shop serving local enclosure designers and PCB assembly houses, then expanded into full fabrication as demand for finished parts grew.

The 15,000 m² facility now houses fibre laser cutting, CNC turret punching, multi-axis press brakes, MIG/TIG welding stations, and automated powder coating. Every stage from flat blank to finished assembly is managed in-house, which allows Weichuang to control lead times and quality without coordinating multiple subcontractors.

Certification includes ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and IATF 16949 for automotive customers. The company supplies precision enclosures and brackets to telecom OEMs, medical device manufacturers, and industrial control companies across Europe and North America. DFM feedback is provided with every quotation to help customers reduce cost and improve manufacturability.

Prototype lead time is 7 days from drawing approval. Production lead time for standard parts is 20–30 days. Volume capacity exceeds 2 million pieces per year. Custom colours, finishes, and hardware insertion are all handled on site.
